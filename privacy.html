Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Summary: LinguaKey does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not have servers. There is no analytics, no tracking, and no user accounts.

Overview

LinguaKey is a keyboard extension that uses AI to transform text. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data LinguaKey accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

LinguaKey does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We do not operate any servers. There is no analytics, no tracking, and no user accounts.

On-Device AI

When using on-device AI mode (Apple Intelligence or local language models), all text processing happens entirely on your device. No text, prompts, or results leave your device.

Cloud AI (BYO Key)

If you choose to configure your own API key for a third-party AI provider (such as OpenAI or OpenRouter), your text is sent directly to that provider's servers using your personal API key. LinguaKey does not route, store, or have access to this traffic. You are subject to the third-party provider's privacy policy when using this feature.

Full Access Permission

iOS requires "Full Access" permission for certain keyboard features. LinguaKey uses Full Access solely for:

  • On-device speech recognition — for dictation input in Ask AI mode
  • Cloud AI requests — only when you have configured your own API key

No network requests are made unless you explicitly use the cloud AI feature with your own API key.

Data Storage

All app data — including your custom actions, settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults and the App Group container). This data is deleted when you uninstall LinguaKey.

Subscriptions

LinguaKey offers optional in-app subscriptions managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not process payments or store any financial information. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID settings.

Children's Privacy

LinguaKey does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require or support user accounts of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Material changes will be communicated through the app or App Store release notes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@linguakey.app.
